# Commercialization Notes

## What creates edge

This product becomes commercially interesting when it does three things better than a normal dashboard:

1. separates reality from resolution
2. ranks only the few signals that matter financially
3. shows where market pricing is most likely wrong

## Why this matters

Many geopolitical prediction products collapse everything into one probability. This system does not. It explicitly models:

- real de-escalation
- qualifying public announcement odds
- the friction between them

That makes it more suitable for resolution-sensitive markets.

## Current monetizable features

- live model-vs-market discrepancy
- candidate signal projected impact
- catalyst feed
- thresholded alerts
- replay for post-trade review

## Best next commercial upgrades

1. Full-article semantic parsing with quote extraction and speaker attribution
2. Replay attribution that shows whether the model was early or late versus market
3. Calibration of actionability scores against historical gap closure and real trade logs
4. Tiered alerting for different trader styles
5. Source health and premium-source integrations

## Honest limitation

More feeds alone do not create a business. Better conversion from information to action creates the business.

The key product question is not:

> "Can we ingest more headlines?"

It is:

> "Can we identify the few updates that should change a position?"
