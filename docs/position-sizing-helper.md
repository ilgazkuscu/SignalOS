# Position Sizing Helper

## Purpose

Provide internal risk discipline so strong ideas do not automatically become oversized positions.

## Rubric

- `FULL`: high trade score, manageable wording risk, near catalyst
- `HALF`: good trade score with one meaningful constraint
- `SMALL`: some edge, but timing/liquidity/risk are weaker
- `AVOID`: watch or no-trade setup

## Disclaimer language

This is internal sizing guidance only. It is not investment advice.

## Configuration options

Sizing tiers live in `apps/web/src/lib/decision/config.ts` and can be tuned without changing presentation code.
