# Limitations

- The system is rules-based, not a learned geopolitical world model.
- Evidence quality depends on source quality and paraphrased structured inputs.
- Deduplication is headline-similarity based, not full semantic clustering.
- Scenario probabilities are disciplined heuristics, not calibrated forecasts.
- Market mapping is relevance-driven, not a causal guarantee of settlement.

Trip delay is reported; cancellation is NOT confirmed.
